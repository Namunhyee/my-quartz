## 발문

# 해설

**정답: ④ 10분자**

NADH 계산:

**Glycolysis (glucose → 2 pyruvate):**
- GAPDH (G-3-P → 1,3-BPG): **+2 NADH**

**Pyruvate oxidation (2 pyruvate → 2 acetyl-CoA):**
- Pyruvate dehydrogenase: **+2 NADH** (× 2분자)

**TCA cycle (2 acetyl-CoA → 4 CO₂):**
각 1회전: 3 NADH + 1 FADH₂ + 1 ATP
- Isocitrate dehydrogenase: +1 NADH
- α-KG dehydrogenase: +1 NADH
- Malate dehydrogenase: +1 NADH
- **1회전 = 3 NADH × 2회전 = +6 NADH**

**총 NADH:**
- Glycolysis: 2
- Pyruvate oxidation: 2
- TCA × 2: 6
- **합계 = 10 NADH → ④**

> [!note] FADH₂는 별도로 계산 (TCA 2회전 × 1 = 2 FADH₂)
> 문제는 NADH만 묻고 있으므로 FADH₂는 포함 안 함.
