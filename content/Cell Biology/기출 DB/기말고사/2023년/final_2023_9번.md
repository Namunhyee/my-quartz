# 해설

**정답: ③ Hydroxyacyl CoA dehydrogenase**

β-oxidation 4단계 효소:

| 단계 | 반응 | 효소 | 보조인자 |
|------|------|------|---------|
| ① | Acyl-CoA → trans-Δ²-enoyl-CoA | **Acyl CoA dehydrogenase** | FAD → FADH₂ |
| ② | trans-Δ²-enoyl-CoA → L-3-hydroxyacyl-CoA | **Enoyl CoA hydratase** | H₂O 첨가 |
| **③** | **L-3-hydroxyacyl-CoA → 3-ketoacyl-CoA** | **Hydroxyacyl CoA dehydrogenase** | NAD⁺ → NADH |
| ④ | 3-ketoacyl-CoA → acetyl-CoA + 단축 acyl-CoA | **β-ketothiolase** | CoA-SH |

그림의 ③번 반응: hydroxyacyl-CoA → ketoacyl-CoA (산화) =**Hydroxyacyl CoA dehydrogenase** (NADH 생성)

- ⑤ Acetyl CoA carboxylase는 **지방산 합성** 효소 (분해가 아님) ✗
