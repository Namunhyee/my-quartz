# 해설

이미지는 epithelial cell 간의 junctional complex를 보여주는 그림으로, 각 구조의 기능이 표시되어 있음.

- **a**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Cell junction/Tight junction/00_Tight junction_MOC|Tight junction]] (zonula occludens) — 상피세포 사이의 간극을 밀봉(seals gap between epithelial cells)
- **b**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Cell junction/Cadherin based adhension/Adheres junction/Adheres junction_MOC|Adherens junction]] (zonula adherens) — 한 세포의 actin filament bundle을 이웃 세포와 연결(connects actin filament bundle in one cell with that in the next cell)
- **c**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Cell junction/Cadherin based adhension/Desmosome/Desmosome_MOC|Desmosome]] (macula adherens) — 한 세포의 intermediate filament를 이웃 세포와 연결(connects intermediate filaments in one cell to those in the next cell)
- **d**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Cell junction/Gap junction/00_Gap junction_MOC|Gap junction]] — 세포 간 수용성 소분자 통과 허용(allows the passage of small water-soluble molecules from cell to cell)
- **e**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Matrix Junctions/integrin/integrin_MOC|Focal adhesion]] — 세포의 actin filament를 세포외기질에 고정(anchors actin filaments in cell to extracellular matrix)
- **f**: [[Cell Biology/내용/19. Cell Junctions and the Extracellular Matrix/Cell Junctions/Cell-Matrix Junctions/hemidesmosomes|Hemidesmosome]] — 세포의 intermediate filament를 세포외기질에 고정(anchors intermediate filaments in a cell to extracellular matrix)
