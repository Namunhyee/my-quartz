# 해설

**정답: 4CO₂ + 2 GTP + 3 FADH₂ + 7 NADH**

계산:
- 부티르산(C4) → butyryl-CoA (ATP 소비, 2 ATP 당량)
- β-oxidation 1 cycle (C4→2 acetyl-CoA): 1 FADH₂ + 1 NADH + 2 acetyl-CoA
- 2 acetyl-CoA → TCA × 2: 각 1 GTP + 1 FADH₂ + 3 NADH + 2 CO₂
- 합계: GTP = 2, FADH₂ = 1+2 = 3, NADH = 1+6 = 7, CO₂ = 4 ✓
