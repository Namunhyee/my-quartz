## 대사 흐름 요약

```
Glucose (cytosol)
  → Glycolysis → 2 Pyruvate + 2 NADH + 2 ATP
  
Fatty acids (cytosol)
  → Activation → Acyl CoA (미토콘드리아)
  → Beta-oxidation → Acetyl CoA + NADH + FADH₂

Pyruvate (미토콘드리아 matrix)
  → Pyruvate oxidation → Acetyl CoA + NADH + CO₂

Acetyl CoA
  → Citric Acid Cycle → NADH + FADH₂ + GTP + CO₂

NADH, FADH₂
  → Electron Transport Chain → Proton gradient
  → ATP synthase → ATP
```
