# Step 6 - Succinate Dehydrogenase

## 반응

**Succinate + FAD → Fumarate + FADH₂**

```
Succinate + FAD  ──(Succinate dehydrogenase)──→  Fumarate + FADH₂
```

## 핵심 정보

| 항목 | 내용 |
|------|------|
| **Reactant** | Succinate (C4), FAD |
| **Product** | Fumarate (C4), FADH₂ |
| **Enzyme** | Succinate dehydrogenase |
| **산물 에너지** | FADH₂ (~1.5 ATP) |

## 중요성: TCA Cycle과 ETC의 연결 효소

Succinate dehydrogenase는 **이중 역할**:
- TCA cycle의 Step 6 효소
- **ETC의 Complex II** (Succinate dehydrogenase) = 미토콘드리아 inner membrane에 내장

> "There is an unusual additional respiratory chain complex that is an important component of the citric acid cycle, where it is known as succinate dehydrogenase. This membrane-embedded enzyme captures electrons during the conversion of succinate to fumarate and passes these electrons directly into the electron-transport chain via a flavin electron carrier (FAD)." — Chapter 14

## 특징

- FAD를 electron acceptor로 사용 (산화 정도가 작아 NAD⁺보다 낮은 redox potential)
- 전자를 ubiquinone으로 직접 전달 → Complex III로 이동
- **Proton pump 아님**: Complex II는 proton을 pumping하지 않음
