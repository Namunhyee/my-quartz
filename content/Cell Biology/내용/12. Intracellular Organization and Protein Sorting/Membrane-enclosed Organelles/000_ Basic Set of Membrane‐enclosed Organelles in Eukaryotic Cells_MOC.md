# [[Nucleus]]
contains genome; site of DNA and RNA synthesis
# [[Cytoplasm]]
 - Cytosol ~50% of cell volume 
 - Protein synthesis and degradation
 - Small molecule degradation
 - Synthesis of building blocks
# [[Endoplasmic Reticulum]](ER)
- Rough ER : Ribosome‐bound; synthesizes membrane & secretory proteins.
- Smooth ER : Lipid synthesis, Ca²⁺ storage
# [[Golgi apparatus_MOC|Golgi apparatus]]
Modifies, sorts, and dispatches proteins and lipids from ER
# [[Mitochondria]]
ATP synthesis
# [[Chloroplasts]]
ATP synthesis; chloroplast also store food/pigment
# [[Lysosome]]
Digestion of defunct intracellular organelles, macromolecules, and particles
# [[Endosome]]
Sorting compartments for endocytosed material
# [[Peroxisome]]
oxidative reactions
